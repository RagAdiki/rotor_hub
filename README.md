# rotor_hub
Designed and developed a swashplateless micro-helicopter inspired by the Black Hornet nano-UAV, achieving cyclic rotor control without conventional swashplate mechanisms through linearly coupled lag–flap hinge geometry. Derived coupled equations of motion on Mathematica, implemented nonlinear MATLAB/Simulink simulations for hover stability validation, and integrated a magnetic encoder to synchronise sinusoidal pitch inputs with blade azimuthal position for controlled rotor disc tilt. Evaluated multiple ESC platforms and motor signal protocols, validated structural integrity via FEA and modal analysis, and correlated bench-top thrust and torque measurements with simulation results.
